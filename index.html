Lotus Sutra

妙法莲华经

Mahayana · 大乘

The Lotus Sutra is among the most revered scriptures in Mahayana Buddhism. Through parables, visions, and soaring imagery, it proclaims a single revolutionary teaching: every being, without exception, possesses the seed of Buddhahood and will one day attain full awakening. Its central message — 一乘法, the One Vehicle — dissolves the boundaries between the different paths and declares that all roads lead to the same summit.
